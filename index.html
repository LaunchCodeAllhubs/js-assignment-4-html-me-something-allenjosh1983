The Rounds: My Favorite Spots To Hang In Kansas City

Chez Charlie | Lucky Boys | Easy Inn | Up Down | Minibar |

Chez Charlie

"Chez Charlie" is one of the last places in "midtown" where you can still grab a cheap drink and meet some quality folks. From the Dj's to the bartenders, to the 20 something's toking on the back patio. Chez Charlie is full to the brim with personality and mystery. Every dartboard, table, and barstool tells a story of nights past. If you find yourself in midwtown, don't hesitate to pop in and contribute to the laughter and the legacy!

Lucky Boys

IYKYK! If you don't, you gotta check this joint out! This revitalized gem lies deep in the heart of Kansas City's Historic West Bottoms. Beaming with charm from the moment you walk in, it doesn't take long to realize it's not just a clever name. This is place actually blessed by "Velvet Jesus". If it's not legendary pizza night, you can feast on many of the LB fan favorites. From the Hot Chicken sando, to the Spicy Cubano, you can't miss. Pull up and catch a cold one coming down the line and have yourself a good time.

Easy Inn

Formerly known as the Colonial Club. This "Stawberry Hill" dive plays home to many local artists and musicians looking to for fun and shenanigans on the Kansas side. Just over the stateline in one of the most Historic neighborhoods in KCK we can find anything from a great set of wings to a great punk show. Add this diverse watering hole to your repotoire immediately!

Up Down

The best place in KC to relive the glory days of the 80's, 90's, and early 2000's is none othr than Up Down Kc. Grab a six pack and a lb of tolkens and head down memory lane, grab a slice of some of the best "zza" in they city or challenge a friend to a a game of skee-ball. The cultivated world at Up Down is a your fun filled Oyester! With over 60 craft beers on tap and great rooftop patio ta boot... Up Down is your place for fun in cowtown.

Minibar

Where else can you catch a killer indie rock show and get a well crafted cocktail in midtown? I'll wait.... If you said Minibar, youre right! This locally owned rock club doubles as a cocktail loung and a kareoke bar. Options! Options! Options! is the name of the game at "mini". With two floors to explore, you can get carried away in a night of multifacited party shenanigans. Next time you're out ditch then norm and give "mini" a shot.
